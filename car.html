Dreamscape

You and several of your friends were on your way to film a video for a competition, but suddenly, you find yourselves in the middle of a wasteland. The sky is dusty and you cannot tell whether it is day or night, and there is nothing or no one around as far as you can see. Suddenly, you feel the car lurch. Not wanting to be lost without a working car, you investigate, only to find your tire has been punctured.

To replace your tire, tap on each nail until it's secure. If even one nail isn't secure, the car will stop working!
